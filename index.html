About me

I've been passionate about video games for as long as I can remember. As a child, I spent hours exploring virtual worlds, solving complex puzzles and challenging my friends to wild competitions. This passion led me to a career as a game designer, where I apply my creativity and passion to create memorable interactive experiences.

I got my start in game design through YouTube tutorials ( Tuto Unity fr , Smart Poly , ... ), where I acquired a solid grounding in game design, interactive storytelling and programming.

As a game designer, my ultimate goal is to create games that captivate players and transport them into extraordinary worlds. I'm always striving to push the boundaries of interactive storytelling and user experience, constantly looking for new ways to innovate and surprise players.

Whether it's designing new game mechanics, writing compelling stories or fine-tuning the balance of gameplay, I'm committed to creating exceptional gaming experiences that will remain etched in players' memories. I'm passionate about the future of video games and look forward to continuing to contribute to this dynamic and constantly evolving industry.
